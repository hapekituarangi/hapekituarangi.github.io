SPRINT 1 - TIME AND HABITS

Time and Habits

27.FEB.2016
Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I tried the Pomodoro and Timebox technique’s.

What did you find interesting?

I felt most of the resources were useful in way or another. However one of favourites was the “Make it big by starting small” article.

What worked for you?

Toggl worked really well for me this week. I found looking at the report summary every night really helpful. It allowed me to easily assess how I had been using my time that day, and see if there were any patterns or habits growing.

Which will you experiment further with?

I plan to experiment further with the Pomodoro technique and some of the "Making it big by starting small" concepts. My aim is to implement the 6 step process of Pomodoro technique with a time limit that works best for me.
